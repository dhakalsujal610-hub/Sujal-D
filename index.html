Biography

A Decade of Healing Work

Keanne Owens is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with Qualified Supervisor designation (LCSW-QS) based in Florida. With over ten years of dedicated practice, she has supported hundreds of individuals through some of life's most difficult challenges — including trauma, anxiety, depression, grief, identity, immigration stress, and relationship difficulties.

Keanne earned her Master of Social Work (MSW) degree and has built a practice centered on the belief that every person carries the seeds of their own healing. Her role as a therapist is to create the conditions in which growth naturally unfolds.

Her clinical background spans community mental health, private practice, and cross-cultural therapeutic work. She has a deep commitment to serving diverse populations, including immigrant communities, BIPOC clients, and first-generation Americans navigating cultural identity and mental health.
